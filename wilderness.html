Once there was…
Once there was a man and woman engaged to be married. But just before their wedding day, a car crash put the woman into a coma. For months, the man sat silently at his fiancee's side until an inner burning compelled him to speak...
1.I - In silence I…
In silence I have sat beside your bed,
but now I beg your sleeping body rise
and quit the dark horizon of the dead,
and harmonize the chords of my reprise.
You're here and yet a thousand miles away;
you're body's here with me yet lost at sea.
I'd dig my arms into your body's clay;
I'd swim the ocean depths to set you free.
But could I strike the heart within your breast
or find my treasure in that dark abyss?
I pray my words, your ears they might arrest;
I pray my whispered breaths won't go amiss.
For here I have to trust my whispered breath
to steal you back and save you from your death.
